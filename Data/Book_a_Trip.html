Are you booking for an unaccompanied minor? Unaccompanied minor should be 7-14 years old. Please note that they may be accommodated on the flight provided that they pay the UM Handling Fee at point of booking and furnish us with the complete necessary documents. The UM form may be downloaded on this website under the 'Customer Service' section.
If you intend to book for a child with an accompanying adult, please do not proceed. Kindly restart your booking and book the child and adult together in one transaction. If you are booking for more than 2 unaccompanied children, please call the hotline for assistance.
Guest’s age should be 7-14 years old to be accommodated as an unaccompanied minor. Age of your guest/s must match the age criteria for the passenger types. Adults should be 12 and over, children are age 2 to below 12 and infants are 8 days old to below 2 years old. Please book them accordingly.
If you intend to book for an infant, please make sure to indicate the accompanying adult. Kindly restart your booking and book the adult and infant together in one transaction.
